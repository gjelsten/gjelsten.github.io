Privacy Policy — Mileage Run Tracker

← Back to Mileage

Effective Date

25 September 2025

1. Introduction

Mileage Run Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the App.

2. Data We Access

The App integrates with Apple Health. We request access only to:

  • Recent running workouts
  • Heart rate (if available)
  • Sleep data
  • VO₂ max

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the App's functionality:

  • Display mileage charts
  • Provide race predictions
  • Show in-depth health statistics

All processing occurs locally on your device.

4. What We Don't Do

  • We do not store your data on our servers.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not sell or monetize your data.
  • We do not upload your data to iCloud or other cloud services.

5. Your Control

  • You can choose which data to share in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.
  • You can revoke access at any time without affecting Apple Health data.
  • Deleting the App completely removes its access to Health data.

6. Security

All data is protected by Apple's HealthKit security, including encryption and biometric authentication.
