Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects and uses information about visitors to the site. The policy contains information you are entitled to know when information is collected from the website (Section 19 of the Norwegian Personal Data Act) and general information about personal data (Section 18, first paragraph, of the Personal Data Act).

This website uses Google Analytics to analyze visitor patterns while ensuring complete anonymity. Although cookies are used, they do not store any personally identifiable information, such as IP addresses. No data can be traced back to individual users.

Examples of collected data include:
The number of visitors to different pages.
The duration of visits.
Navigation patterns between pages.

For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
