Western Norway: Cinematic and Ever-Changing

Exploring the dramatic landscapes that inspire my work and shape my visual style

Sunnmøre isn't just beautiful—it's made for storytelling. From the dramatic Sunnmøre Alps to quiet fjords and coastal villages, the light and seasons here constantly transform the landscape.

It's a filmmaker's dream—one of the most stunning and versatile locations in the world to bring stories to life.
