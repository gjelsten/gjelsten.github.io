Mileage Run Tracker

Mileage Run Tracker gives you clear, detailed mileage charts, accurate race predictions, and in-depth health statistics — all powered by Apple Health. Whether you're a beginner, preparing for your first 5K, or training for a marathon, the app keeps you focused, consistent, and motivated.

Features

  • Mileage charts: Weekly, monthly, and yearly running charts.

Premium Features:

  • Race predictions: Get accurate finish time predictions based on your recent workouts and performance trends.
  • Health stats: Track heart rate trends, VO₂ max, sleep patterns, and comprehensive health metrics.
  • Widgets: iPhone and Apple Watch widgets.

Support

No data visible? Check Apple Health permissions: Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Mileage.

Runs showing in wrong metric? Change to either km or miles in Mileage app settings. On Apple Watch, there is a dedicated setting directly in the app.

Subscription issues? Manage subscriptions via Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
