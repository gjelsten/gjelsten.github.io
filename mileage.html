Mileage Run Tracker

Mileage gives you clear, beautiful mileage charts, accurate race predictions, and in-depth health stats — all powered by Apple Health.

Whether you’re a beginner or training for a marathon, it keeps you focused, consistent, and motivated.

Support

No data? Check Apple Health permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Mileage.

Watch not syncing? Ensure both devices are connected and Health app is syncing.

Subscription issues? Manage through Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

Privacy Policy

Your Data Stays Private

Your running data stays on your device. We only read from Apple Health and never share your data.

What We Access

We request access to your Apple Health data including:

  • Recent Running workouts
  • Heart rate (if available)
  • Sleep data
  • VO2 max

This data is used only to show your running statistics and progress within the app.

What We Don't Do

  • No data collection - Nothing is stored on our servers
  • No sharing - Your data is never shared with anyone
  • No selling - We don't sell or monetize your information
  • No cloud storage - Everything stays on your iPhone

Your Control

  • Choose which data to share through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health
  • Revoke access anytime without affecting your Health app data
  • Delete the app to completely remove access

Security

Your data is protected by Apple's HealthKit security, including encryption and biometric authentication. All processing happens locally on your device.
