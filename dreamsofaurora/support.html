Support

Having trouble with audio playback? Make sure your device volume is turned up and that Do Not Disturb mode isn't interfering with audio playback.

Stories not loading? The app works offline, but if you're experiencing issues, try restarting the app or your device.

App crashing or freezing? Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, try restarting your device.

Subscription issues? Manage subscriptions via Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
